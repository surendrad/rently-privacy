Overview

Rently is a property management application designed for individual landlords and small property owners. This Privacy Policy explains what information Rently collects, how it is used, where it is stored, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Rently, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Rently collects only the information you explicitly enter into the app. We do not collect telemetry, analytics, advertising identifiers, or any data beyond what is needed to provide the app's core functionality.

Account Information

Your email address and password, used to create and authenticate your Rently account.

Property and Financial Data

Property addresses, unit details, mortgage amounts, lender names, annual property tax, insurance premiums, and expense records including attached receipt images.

Tenant and Tenancy Data

Tenant names, phone numbers, and email addresses. Lease terms including move-in and move-out dates, rent amounts, security deposits, rent due dates, and payment history.

Receipt Images

Photos taken with your device camera or selected from your photo library, uploaded as compressed image files to your private cloud storage folder.

Communications

Messages you compose to tenants, stored locally and synced to your account.

How We Use Your Information

All data you enter is used solely to provide Rently's property management features:

  • Displaying your properties, units, tenants, and financials within the app
  • Calculating rent rolls, monthly summaries, and annual financial reports
  • Generating alerts for lease expirations, overdue rent, and rent increase reminders
  • Syncing your data across devices when you are signed in
  • Attaching receipt images to expense records for your records

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. The app works fully offline and cloud sync is optional.

Cloud Storage

When you enable cloud sync, your data is stored on Supabase (Supabase Inc.), a managed cloud database platform hosted on AWS. Your data is protected by strict access controls that ensure only your authenticated account can read or write your records. All data is transmitted over HTTPS at all times.

Receipt images are stored in a private storage bucket. Files are not publicly accessible and are tied strictly to your account. Your password is never stored in plain text.

Third-Party Services

Rently uses one third-party service for cloud functionality:

Service Provider Purpose Privacy Policy
Supabase Supabase Inc. Cloud database, authentication, and file storage supabase.com/privacy

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any other third-party data collection tools.

Device Permissions

Camera

Used only when you choose to photograph a receipt to attach to an expense record. The camera is never accessed in the background.

Photo Library and Files

Used only when you choose to select an existing image to attach to an expense record.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party, except:

  • Supabase Inc., as the infrastructure provider for cloud sync and storage
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or court order

Your tenant data is never shared with any external party.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the app.

Cloud data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing surendra.devarashetty@gmail.com. Requests are processed within 30 days.

Receipt images are deleted from cloud storage when you remove them from an expense record.

Children's Privacy

Rently is a business application intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data you have entered — it is visible directly within the app
  • Export your data using the built-in CSV export feature
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app or contacting us to remove your cloud account
  • Correct any data by editing records directly within the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
